About Us

The Green City web platform provides a platform for users to lodge complaints about issues related to garbage, sewage, and drainage. Users can navigate to the "Complaints" section of the website and fill out a form detailing the nature of the problem and its location. The form may require the user to provide additional information, such as photos or videos, to help the authorities understand the issue better. Once submitted, the complaint will be forwarded to the relevant department, and the user will receive a confirmation email. The authorities will investigate the issue and take appropriate action to resolve it. The Green City web platform aims to ensure that users' complaints are addressed promptly, and the city remains clean and healthy.
