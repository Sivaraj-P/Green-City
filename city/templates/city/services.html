Services


As an organization, we take the health and safety of our community very seriously. We understand that garbage, sewage, and stagnant water can pose a threat to the well-being of individuals and the environment. That is why we have a dedicated team of workers who are responsible for addressing any complaints related to these issues. Our workers are trained and equipped with the necessary tools to effectively resolve any concerns that may arise. We encourage members of our community to reach out to us with any complaints they may have, as we are committed to providing a clean and safe environment for all. Our service types are listed below.


Garbage

The garbage was left uncollected and piled up, emitting a foul odor and becoming a breeding ground for pests and diseases. The accumulation of trash also posed environmental hazards and created an eyesore for the surrounding community.

Sewage

Sewage leaking is a serious public health and environmental hazard that can lead to contamination of water sources and the spread of diseases. It is essential to promptly identify and address sewage leaks to minimize their harmful effects.

Sanitization

Improper sanitization can lead to the spread of harmful germs and bacteria, increasing the risk of infections and illnesses. It is essential to maintain proper sanitization practices in all areas, including homes, workplaces, and public spaces.

Manhole

The open manhole poses a significant danger to pedestrians and drivers alike, as it can cause severe injuries or even death if someone were to fall inside. It is important to report any open manholes immediately to the relevant authorities to ensure public safety.

Burning of Garbage

Burning of garbage releases harmful pollutants into the air, causing air pollution and health problems for nearby residents. It is a dangerous and unsustainable method of waste disposal that should be avoided in favor of safer and more environmentally-friendly alternatives.

Water Stangnation

Water stagnation on roads can lead to several problems such as flooding, increased risk of accidents, and damage to vehicles. It is important to address the issue promptly to ensure the safety and convenience of commuters.
